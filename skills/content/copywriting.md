# Copywriting Skill

## When to Use This Skill

Activate when the user needs to write, rewrite, or review copy for landing pages, emails, or ads.

Read `product-marketing.md` first.

---

## Core Principles
1. **Clarity over cleverness**: If they have to think about what the headline means, they will bounce.
2. **You > We**: Count the number of times you say "You" vs "We" or "Our". The ratio should be at least 3:1 in favor of "You".
3. **Benefits > Features**: Features tell what it does. Benefits sell what it means for the user.
4. **Specifics sell**: "Save 4 hours a week" beats "Save time".

## The "Rule of One"
Every piece of copy should have:
- ONE core emotion
- ONE primary benefit
- ONE single call to action
