# Ad Creative Skill

## When to Use This Skill

Activate when the user needs to brainstorm, draft, or review ad creative concepts, scripts, or visuals across any platform.

Read `product-marketing.md` first.

---

## Core Creative Frameworks

### 1. AIDA Framework
- **Attention**: Hook them immediately.
- **Interest**: Keep them watching/reading.
- **Desire**: Show the transformation.
- **Action**: Tell them what to do next.

### 2. PAS Framework
- **Problem**: Identify the exact pain point.
- **Agitation**: Make the pain feel urgent.
- **Solution**: Present your product as the only logical fix.

### 3. The 3-Second Hook Rule
If it's video, the first 3 seconds must answer:
- Who is this for?
- Why should they care?
- What's the payoff for watching?

## Creative Angles to Test
Always propose testing multiple angles:
1. **The Founder Story**: Why was this built?
2. **Us vs. Them**: Compare to the old way.
3. **Mythbusting**: Challenge a common industry belief.
4. **The Unboxing/Demo**: Show, don't tell.
